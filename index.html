Case Study: The Rushed Sterilisation

Alex, a dental assistant, is rushing and doesn't properly clean instruments before putting them in the steriliser. They skip the crucial step of scrubbing off visible debris from the instruments.

Harm and Consequences

  • Patient Safety

    Instruments aren't sterile, risking infection transmission (e.g., Hepatitis) to patients.

  • Individual Repercussions

    Alex may face disciplinary action for not following WHS and safe work practices.

  • Practice Liability

    The dental practice could face investigation, significant fines, and severe reputational damage for breaching infection control standards.

  • Legal Action

    If a patient becomes sick, they can take legal action against the practice for negligence, seeking compensation.
